The Evaluation Charter

Anonymized Double-Blind Matrix

The Nexus Academic Committee operates entirely on an autonomous, student-led peer evaluation system. To secure absolute objective integrity and combat structural privilege or institutional favoritism, the platform strictly executes a Double-Blind Review Protocol.

Upon transmission routing, every manuscript is automatically stripped of personal identities, geographical telemetry, and institutional metrics. Submissions are judged solely on analytical consistency, structural logic, empirical evidence, and the effectiveness of the counter-argument defense.
